Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement explains our practices, including your preferences and choices, regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of certain information, including your personal data, in connection with the Netflix service.

How to contact us

If you have general questions about your account or how to contact our customer service, visit our online help center at help.netflix.com. If you have concerns about this Privacy Statement or the use of your personal data, cookies, or similar technologies, you can write to our Data Protection Officer/Privacy Department at privacy@netflix.com.

Netflix International B.V. and Netflix Servicios de Transmisión España, S.L. (help.netflix.com/corpinfo) are joint controllers of your personal data. Both are responsible for protecting your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Statement, and you can exercise your data protection rights against either of these data controllers. To ask questions or exercise your rights, you can use the contact details mentioned above. Please note that if you contact us for assistance, for your safety and ours, we may need to verify your identity before addressing your request.

Information Collection

We receive and store information about you, including:

  • Information you provide:

    We obtain information you provide, including:
    • your name, email address, payment method(s), phone number, and any other identifier you may use (such as your in-game name). If you have subscribed to an ad-supported plan, gender and date of birth are also included. We obtain this information in various ways, including when you share it using our service, contact our customer service, or participate in surveys or marketing promotions;
    • information obtained if you decide to post ratings, likes, and preferences, your account settings (including your preferences defined in the 'Account' section of our website), or when you provide information through our service or otherwise.

  • Automatically obtained information:

    We obtain information about you and your use of our service, your interactions with us and our advertising, as well as information related to your network, network devices, your computer, or other Netflix-compatible devices you use to access our service (such as gaming consoles, smart TVs, mobile phones, decoders, and other streaming devices). This information includes:
    • your activity on the Netflix service: title selections, series you have watched, ads viewed (if applicable), search queries, and your activity on Netflix Games;
    • your interactions with our emails and SMS, and with our messages sent through push notifications and our online messaging channels;
    • details of your interactions with our customer service, such as the date, time, and reason for contacting us, transcripts of any chat conversations, and, if you call us, your phone number and recordings of the calls;
    • device IDs or other unique identifiers, including those of your network devices (such as your router) and Netflix-compatible devices connected to your network;
    • resettable device identifiers (also called "advertising identifiers"), such as those of mobile devices, tablets, and streaming devices that include such identifiers (for more information, see the "Cookies and Internet Advertising" section below);
    • device and software features (such as type and configuration), connection information (including type: Wi-Fi, mobile data, etc.), page view statistics, referral origins (URLs, for example), IP address (which may indicate your approximate location, such as city, state or province, and postal code), browser type, operating system, and device type;
    • information collected through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other technologies, including advertising data (such as information on the availability and delivery of ads, the sites on which they were served, and the ads that were clicked), user purchasing history, and your interests (which we infer based on your interactions with us).

Please note: To provide our service, we do not intentionally collect sensitive information about you, such as information related to your racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or trade union membership. Where required by law, we will obtain your explicit consent to collect and process such information.

Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • provide, analyze, improve, promote, and personalize our service;
  • troubleshoot software issues and operational problems;
  • learn about our users and their preferences;
  • communicate with you;
  • operate our business, develop innovative features, and provide customer support;
  • ensure the security and integrity of our service;
  • enforce our terms (including determining any violations);
  • evaluate and improve the effectiveness of our marketing and advertising campaigns and strategies;
  • prevent fraud and unauthorized use of our service, and for other purposes that we may notify to you when we request your information.

We use automated systems that analyze your content to provide you with recommendations, personalized content, and advertising based on your interests. We also use automated systems to analyze the content of communications between users to detect and prevent fraud, to provide a more personalized experience, and for other purposes described in this Privacy Statement.

In the case of Netflix Games, we will also use your information to provide you with game recommendations, personalized content, and advertising based on your interests, as well as to analyze the content of communications between users to detect and prevent fraud and provide a more personalized experience.

Your Choices

You have several choices regarding the use of information on our service:

  • Email and Text Messages

    If you no longer want to receive certain communications from us, simply access the 'Communication Settings' section of your 'Account' page and unsubscribe from the relevant communication.

  • Push Notifications

    If you receive push notifications on your mobile device and no longer wish to receive them, you can disable them in your device settings.

  • Interest-Based Ads

    If you do not want us to use information that we collect or that you provide to us to provide you with interest-based ads, you can choose not to receive them by selecting the appropriate option in your 'Communication Settings' (for subscribers) or 'Profile' (for non-subscribers).

  • Localization Features

    If you have opted to share your location with us, you can always revoke this permission by changing the location settings on your device.

If you have opted to share your location details with us, you can always revoke this permission by changing the location settings on your device.
